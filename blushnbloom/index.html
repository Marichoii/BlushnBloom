About

Blush & Bloom is a personal and academic project developed with the purpose of exploring how design and technology can assist users in making more informed and confident beauty decisions. Born from a desire to unite aesthetic sense with practical guidance, this platform offers personalized insights into makeup selection, color theory, skin undertone identification, and hair color harmony.

The project was motivated by the observation that many people feel overwhelmed or unsure when choosing beauty products that truly enhance their features. By combining visual analysis, colorimetry principles, and user-friendly design, Blush & Bloom seeks to simplify this process and deliver tailored recommendations based on each individual's natural characteristics.

Whether for casual routines or special occasions, our goal is to provide a smart, inclusive, and aesthetically refined experience — turning beauty into a tool for self-expression and confidence.
